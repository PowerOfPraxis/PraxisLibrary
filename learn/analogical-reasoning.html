Analogical Reasoning

Solve new problems by drawing parallels to familiar ones.

The Concept

Learning from Similarity

Analogical reasoning prompts the model to recall relevant examples from its training and apply similar solutions to new problems. By asking "What similar problems have been solved before?", the model can transfer strategies across domains.

The Power of Analogies

Just as humans learn by connecting new concepts to familiar ones, language models can leverage pattern matching across their training data to approach novel problems more effectively.

Prompt Pattern

"Think of similar problems you've seen before. What approaches worked for those? Apply a similar strategy here."

Application

When to Use Analogical Reasoning

Novel Problems

When facing an unusual problem, prompt the model to find structural similarities to known problems.

Cross-Domain Transfer

Apply solutions from one field to another by highlighting parallels.

Creative Solutions

Generate innovative approaches by combining strategies from analogous situations.
