System 2 Attention (S2A)

Filter distractions by regenerating focused context.

The Concept

Deliberate Focus

System 2 Attention addresses the problem of LLMs being distracted by irrelevant information in their context. Before answering, the model first regenerates the context to include only relevant information, then reasons on this cleaned version.

Named After

The "System 2" in psychology refers to slow, deliberate, analytical thinkingâ€”in contrast to fast, automatic "System 1" responses.

Two-Step Process

1) Rewrite context keeping only task-relevant information. 2) Answer based on the filtered context.

Example

S2A in Action

Original Context (Biased)

"John is a brilliant, award-winning chef. Mary is an average home cook. Both made chocolate cake. Whose cake tastes better?"

S2A Approach

Step 1 - Regenerate: "John and Mary both made chocolate cake."

Step 2 - Answer: "Without tasting both cakes or having quality information, I cannot determine which tastes better."

Use Cases

When S2A Helps

Sycophancy Prevention

Remove flattery or leading statements that bias the model toward certain answers.

Distraction Removal

Filter out irrelevant details that could mislead reasoning in complex prompts.

Fair Evaluation

Strip away reputation or prior beliefs to evaluate options objectively.
