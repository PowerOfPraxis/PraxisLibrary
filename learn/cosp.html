Self-Adaptive

COSP

Build demonstrations from the model's own consistent outputs.

The Concept

Consistency-based Self-adaptive Prompting

COSP uses the model's own highly-consistent zero-shot outputs as pseudo-demonstrations. Questions where the model shows high agreement across samples are assumed reliable and used as few-shot examples for harder questions.

Key Insight

High consistency suggests confidence and correctness. These reliable outputs can bootstrap demonstrations without human labeling.

Process

1) Run zero-shot with sampling. 2) Identify high-consistency answers. 3) Use as pseudo-demonstrations. 4) Apply to target questions.

Benefits

Why COSP Works

No Labeling

Generates demonstrations automatically without human annotation effort.

Task-Specific

Demonstrations come from the same task distribution, ensuring relevance.

Quality Filter

Consistency filtering selects likely-correct examples automatically.
