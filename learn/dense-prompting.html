Prompt Design

Dense Prompting

Pack comprehensive context into your prompts.

The Concept

Information-Rich Prompts

Dense Prompting emphasizes including comprehensive context, constraints, and guidance in prompts. Rather than minimal instructions, it provides rich detail that helps the model fully understand the task requirements and nuances.

The Principle

More relevant context generally improves output quality. Dense prompts leave less ambiguity and fewer gaps for the model to fill incorrectly.

What to Include

Context, constraints, examples, format requirements, edge cases, and quality criteria.

Elements

Dense Prompt Components

Background Context

Relevant information about the domain, situation, or user needs.

Explicit Constraints

Clear boundaries on format, length, style, and content restrictions.

Quality Criteria

What makes a good response? Define success explicitly.
