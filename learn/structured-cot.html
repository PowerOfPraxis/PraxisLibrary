Structured Reasoning

Structured Chain-of-Thought

Template-guided reasoning for consistent outputs.

The Concept

Templated Reasoning

Structured CoT provides explicit templates or formats that the model must follow when reasoning. This enforces consistency, makes outputs more predictable, and ensures all required reasoning steps are included.

Why Structure Helps

Templates prevent the model from skipping steps, ensure consistent output format, and make it easier to parse and validate reasoning.

Example Template

Given: [facts]
Goal: [objective]
Steps: [numbered list]
Answer: [result]
