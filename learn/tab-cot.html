Tab-CoT (Tabular Chain-of-Thought)

Organize reasoning steps in structured tables.

The Concept

Tabular Structure

Tab-CoT formats chain-of-thought reasoning as tables with columns for step number, action, reasoning, and intermediate results. This structured format makes reasoning more organized, traceable, and easier to verify.

Benefits

Tables force explicit separation of steps, make it easy to spot errors, and provide clear documentation of the reasoning process.

Common Columns

Step | Operation | Input | Output | Reasoning

Example

Tab-CoT in Action

Problem

"A baker has 48 cupcakes. She sells 1/3, then bakes 12 more. How many does she have?"
