Step-Back Prompting

Zoom out to principles before zooming in to specifics.

The Concept

Big Picture First

Step-Back prompting asks the model to first consider the high-level principles or concepts relevant to a question before attempting to answer the specific query. This abstraction step grounds the response in foundational knowledge.

Why It Works

Specific questions can be misleading or have hidden complexity. By stepping back to principles first, the model ensures its answer is built on solid conceptual foundations.

The Pattern

"Before answering, step back and consider: What general principles are relevant here?"

Example

Step-Back in Action

Specific Question

"What is the temperature of the sun's core?"

Step-Back Approach

Step back: What physical processes occur in stellar cores? What determines core temperature?

Principles: Nuclear fusion, gravitational pressure, hydrostatic equilibrium...

Then answer: With this foundation, provide a grounded response about solar core temperature.
