Long Context

Thread of Thought

Walk through long contexts systematically before answering.

The Concept

Sequential Processing

Thread of Thought (ThoT) handles long documents by explicitly walking through them segment by segment, summarizing and analyzing each part before synthesizing a final answer. This prevents the model from skipping or losing important details.

The Problem

With very long contexts, models may focus on certain parts and miss others. ThoT forces systematic coverage of the entire input.

The Prompt

"Walk me through this context in manageable parts, summarizing key points from each section before answering."

Applications

When to Use ThoT

Long Documents

Research papers, legal documents, or lengthy reports that need comprehensive analysis.

Multi-Source QA

Questions requiring information from multiple parts of a large context.

Summarization

Creating thorough summaries that don't miss key sections.
