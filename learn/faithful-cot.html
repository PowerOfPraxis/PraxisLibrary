Faithful Chain-of-Thought

Ensure reasoning truly supports the conclusion.

The Concept

Faithful Reasoning

Faithful CoT addresses the problem of unfaithful reasoning chainsâ€”where the stated steps don't actually lead to or support the answer. It ensures each reasoning step is genuinely used in deriving the conclusion, not just post-hoc rationalization.

The Problem

Models sometimes generate plausible-looking reasoning that doesn't actually determine the answer. The answer may come from shortcuts while reasoning is decorative.

Faithful Test

If you changed a reasoning step, would the answer change? If not, that step may not be faithfully contributing.

Approach

Ensuring Faithfulness

Symbolic Grounding

Use formal logic or code to ensure steps mechanically determine outcomes.

Step Dependencies

Explicitly require each step to reference and build on previous steps.

Counterfactual Check

Verify that changing steps would change the answer appropriately.
