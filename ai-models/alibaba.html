About Alibaba Cloud (Qwen)

Alibaba Cloud Qwen™

The research team behind the Qwen family, one of the most widely adopted open-weight model families globally, with the broadest language coverage of any frontier lineup.

Mission and Philosophy

Alibaba Cloud’s Qwen team, based in Hangzhou, develops the Qwen series of large language models. Since the first release in 2023, the team has shipped rapid iterations of open-weight models under Apache 2.0 licensing, one of the most permissive licenses in frontier AI, and has built a reputation for strong multilingual performance and transparent research.

Qwen has become a global default for open-source AI research and production deployments. The combination of permissive licensing, broad language coverage, and consistent quality improvements makes it a natural starting point for teams that need frontier capability without the constraints of closed APIs.

The Qwen Lineup

Qwen segments its current lineup around three distinct tiers, each tuned for a specific class of workload:

  • Qwen3.5 is the current flagship, a next-generation 397B MoE with 17B active parameters that supports 201 languages, the broadest language coverage in any open-weight frontier model.
  • Qwen3 is the prior generation flagship, notable for unifying “thinking” and “non-thinking” modes in a single 235B MoE model with 22B active parameters.
  • QwQ-32B is the dedicated reasoning specialist, a smaller model tuned for extended chain-of-thought that punches well above its weight class on math and scientific benchmarks.

Open Weights and Licensing

Every current Qwen model ships with open weights under Apache 2.0 licensing, one of the most permissive terms in frontier AI. That means Qwen models can be used commercially, modified, fine-tuned, redistributed, and embedded in proprietary products without scale-based restrictions or usage limits.

Qwen models are available through Alibaba Cloud’s Model Studio API, Hugging Face for direct download, and every major cloud marketplace. The same permissive license applies across all distribution surfaces, making Qwen the most legally clean open-weight option for teams building commercial products.
