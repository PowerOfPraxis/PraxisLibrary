About xAI

xAI™

The AI company behind the Grok model family, known for aggressive compute scaling, the SuperGrok tier, and native integration with the X platform for real-time context.

Mission and Philosophy

xAI was founded by Elon Musk in 2023 with the stated mission to “understand the true nature of the universe.” The company develops the Grok family of AI models and has rapidly scaled its compute infrastructure into one of the largest AI training clusters in the world, located in Memphis.

The Grok models are designed for direct, unfiltered reasoning and are tightly integrated with the X platform, giving them a distinct advantage on real-time context and current events compared to models that rely solely on static training data.

The Grok Lineup

xAI segments its lineup around the Grok-4 generation with three primary surfaces for different use cases:

  • Grok-4 is the flagship, setting multiple benchmark records at launch and available through the xAI API and SuperGrok subscriptions.
  • Grok-4 Heavy is the multi-agent variant that spawns parallel reasoning agents to cross-check findings, available through the SuperGrok Heavy subscription tier.
  • Grok-3 remains the prior-generation flagship, still widely deployed for workloads that were validated on it before Grok-4 shipped.

API Access and Real-Time Context

Grok models are available through xAI’s own API and through the Grok app on the X platform. The tight integration with X gives Grok a unique ability to pull fresh posts, trending topics, and real-time context directly into its responses, useful for workloads that need to reason about current events without building a separate retrieval pipeline.

SuperGrok and SuperGrok Heavy subscriptions unlock higher usage limits and access to the multi-agent Grok-4 Heavy variant. Enterprise API access is available for teams building production integrations on top of Grok.
