About Alibaba Cloud (Qwen)

Alibaba Cloud’s Qwen team develops the Qwen series of large language models, one of the most widely adopted open-weight model families globally. Based in Hangzhou, China, the team has rapidly iterated from Qwen 1.5 through Qwen 2.5, with each generation bringing significant improvements in multilingual understanding, mathematical reasoning, and coding.

Qwen3.5 (2026) is the current flagship, a next-generation open-weights family with the Qwen3.5-397B-A17B supporting 201 languages under Apache 2.0 licensing. Currently the leading open-weight model family, with AIME 2026 scores of 91.3% and MMMU of 85.0%.

Full model family from 0.6B to 235B parameters. Key innovation: unified “thinking” and “non-thinking” mode in a single model. Flagship Qwen3-235B-A22B is a mixture-of-experts model. Source: arxiv.org/html/2505.09388v1.

A dedicated reasoning model using reinforcement learning for extended chain-of-thought. Demonstrates that smaller reasoning-optimized models can compete with much larger general-purpose ones.
